Elephant Grounds Manila

Hong Kong Elephant Grounds has made its way to invest in the Philippine. The Manila branch features an array of menu dishes ranging from their customers favorite Gruyere Grilled Cheese to a sweet plate of Waffle. A la Mode made from locally-sourced ingredients. As for its coffee, their Manila OG Blend is freshly roasted using beans from Costa Rica, Brazil, and Sumatra. Two brewing methods are also available upon request: the Chemex and V60 pour-over.
